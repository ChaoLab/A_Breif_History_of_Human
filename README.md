### 9. The arrow of history 
Myths and fiction created artificial instincts that enables millions of strangers to cooperate effectively. This network of artificial instincts is called culture.  
文化是人为编织的一张观念认知的网。每一种文化的开源都充满着秘密和传说，其实文化就是一种自我适应建立起来的生活观念和方式，开端都很飘渺。但是却能有效的联系人们一起去合作生活。

Every culture has its typical beliefs, norms and values, but these are in constant flux.

Unlike the laws of physics, which are free of inconsistencies, every man-made order is packed with internal contradictions. Cultures are constantly trying to reconcile these contradictions, and this process fuels change.  
除了物理定律以外，只要是人所指定的规则，那么就会包涵内在的矛盾（这是不是和马克思主义哲学里面对立统一的原则是一样的?）。中国的传统的为人处事的格言里面，比如增广贤文，里面也有一些相反的表述，比如“人无远虑，必有近忧。”和“为人莫做千年计，三十河东四十西。”不同的话，在不同的语境下，在不同的实际情况下，都各自有道理。

中国古代认为耕读是本业，工商是末业。随着时代的发展，工商和科技变成了现在的支柱产业，相应的，农耕文明所孕育出来的农耕文化也会经受内在矛盾的冲击，慢慢的演变成与现实相适应的工业或者数码或者生物科技文化。

Discord in our thoughts, ideas, and values compel us to think, re-evaluate, and criticize.   
就是因为这些存在于文化中的矛盾，激发思考，产生新的观念。文化才不断的演进。

If a Christian really wants to understand Muslim, he shouldn’t look for a pristine set of values that every Muslim holds dear. Rather, he should enquire into the catch-22s of Muslim culture, those places where rules are at war and standards scuffle.  
了解一种文化最好的办法就是进入里面矛盾的地方，一窥其实质。中华文化的矛盾是什么？强调次序，强调稳定，强调忠诚。实际上，越是强调的地方，越是薄弱的地方，人口这么多，在外来文化的冲击下，人心不稳，就会产生社会的大动荡，而这样对每个人来讲都是损失。

人人追求平等，过分的强调平等就损害了既得利益者的自由。要追求儒家道德意义上社会的和谐，那么就会压抑人心，压抑欲望，这是两难的问题。

#### 9.1. The Spy Satellite
The break-ups of culture and language are temporary reversals in an inexorable trend towards unity.  
文化和语言的合并统一是历史的必然。

From such vantage point it becomes crystal clear that history is moving relentlessly towards unity.  
站在以千年为单位的有利历史视角上面，才能看到文化相互影响进而统一的历史进程。

It takes the Afro-Asian giant several centuries to digest all it had swallowed, but the process is irreversible. Today all humans share the same geopolitical system, the same economic system, the same legal system, and the same scientific system.  
所以中医也是科学医学所要洗掉，掩盖掉的一类是似而非的“学科”吗？科学的系统只有一个。而那些有效的中草药只是碰巧（青蒿素）或者只是安慰剂而已？

We still talk about “authentic” cultures, but if by “authentic” we mean something that developed independently, and that consists of ancient local traditions free of external influences, then there are no authentic cultures left on earth. Over the last few centuries, all cultures were changed almost beyond recognition by a flood of global influence.  
文化在不知不觉当中受到外接因素的影响。

电影当中的美洲原住民骑士的形象并不是真的。他们不是古老文化的捍卫者。美洲原住民骑士是17，18世纪发生在西北美洲大草原上一系列军事和政治革命的产物。

#### 9.2. The global vision  
从进化的角度来讲，人类和其他的动物一样，有着“我们”和“他们”之分。但是人类从公元一千年以前开始逐渐发展出来了一种全球的观念。这个观念是从公元前一千年古埃及统一开始逐渐发端的。人类开始合作，统一的帝国开始可以形成。人们开始意识到整个世界和整个人类其实可以被统一到一些基本的规则上去。这样以来所有的人都是“我们”，最为人类这一个整体。三样法宝有全球的整合力，金钱，政治和宗教。    

### 10. The scent of money
Even the catholic bishops of Melgueil and Agde issued these faithful copies of popular Muslim coins, and God-fearing Christians happily used them.
金钱的力量甚至超过了宗教，虽然基督教和伊斯兰教在中世纪的时候是彼此的对头，但是各自还是乐于使用对方的钱币。

#### 10.1. How much is it?
The rise of cities and kingdoms and the improvement in transport infrastructure brought about new opportunities for specialization.
大型的城市和国家的诞生和交通工具的提升带来了社会化大生产的新机遇。这个和马克思主义的学说是一致的。

But specialization created a problem – how do you manage the exchange of goods between the specialists?
社会化分工带来一个问题，如何来管理分工者之间的商品的交换。钱币就是这个交换的中间物。

An economy of favors and obligations doesn’t work when large numbers of strangers try to cooperate.
中国古代的经济基础是封建小农经济，在小范围内自给自足，所以也没有特别大的内在的动力去和外部的世界进行交换。对于整个国家而言，“天朝”物产丰盈，也根本不需要和外邦互通有无，而唯一需要和外邦进行商品交流的事件 – 朝贡 – 也不过是自我显示天朝上国的优越，富足和宽仁的胸怀，礼节性的“慰问”“施舍”给外邦吧。

#### 10.2. Shells and Cigarettes 
More than 90 per cent of all money – more than 50 trillion appearing in our accounts – exists only on computer servers. Accordingly, more business transactions are executed by moving electronic data from one computer to another, without any exchange of physical cash.
想起来其实挺恐怖，一个人一生的劳动，攒下来的钱，其实就是银行终端服务器里面的一串0/1组合。如果被抹去了怎么办？那样的话一生的辛苦，就是个零。好的就是这些东西都有国家的信誉作为保障。如果国家没有信誉怎么办？还好，在全球化的今天，过于那样一抹清零的事情不会发生。

Because money can convert, store, and transport wealth easily and cheaply, it made a vital contribution to the appearance of complex commercial networks and dynamic markets.
交换，储存和转运是货币的最重要的属性。

#### 10.3. How does money work?
Money is the most universal and most efficient system of mutual trust ever devised. 
货币是有史以来被发明的最广泛和最有效的基于互信的系统。

The crucial role of trust explains why our financial systems are so tightly bound up with our political, social and ideological systems, why financial crises are often triggered by political developments, and why the stock market can rise or fall depending on the way traders feel on a particular morning.
社会和政治的不稳定会带来金融危机。同样的，股票市场的升降波动源自于投资者的信心。

Just as writing developed to answer the needs of intensifying administrative activities, so barley money developed to answer the needs of intensifying economic activities.
书写系统的发明是为了更好地管理公共的事务，货币系统的发明是为了更好的增进经济活动。

Set weights of precious metals eventually gave birth to coins.

The coins get imprinted with an identification mark. The mark testified to two things. First, it indicated how much precious metal the coin contained. Second, it identified the authority that issued the coin and that guaranteed its contents. 
中国古代的铜钱和元宝都印有XX通宝的字样，也是官方机构赋予钱币信用的证明，是国家机关以权威来认定的。

那么，同样的一个社会的主流意识形态也是由官方认定和推广的。好比钱币一样，如果我认定，我的邻居也认定，那么这个就是通行，何况还有官方机构的背书。

中国古代儒家思想的形成也是由官方认定以后，再深入社会，随着历史的发展，深入人心。从中央朝廷的官员，到地方上地位优越的士绅，如果大家都认定，那么这样的一种文化，规矩和习惯就会固定下来。如果这个文化能够使国家繁荣，社会安定，人民生活有保障，那么就会更加的促进文化本身的根深蒂固。

外儒内法也就是用表面上的儒家礼仪规矩人伦次序，加上内在的国家强权，来推进国家的意识形态的稳定，没有人能和这样的权威挑战。中华文明一直流传到今天，内在的一些思想和观念（比如稳定次序，人民富足，定于一尊，崇尚道德等等）在今天的社会里面重新被定义，重新被推广。

A long as people trusted the power and integrity of the king, they trusted his coins. In turn, the power of the emperor rested on the denarius (罗马货币的名称)。
权力和金钱相辅相成。国王要收税付工资，在整个帝国内部，分配资金，调动资源，必须要用到易于储存和转运的货币。

#### 10.4. The Gospel of Gold (金钱的福音)
Muslim and European merchants and conquerors gradually spread the Lydian system and the gospel of gold to the far corners of the earth.
像传播福音一样，把以金币为货币的系统传播到世界的每一个角落。

The appearance of a single transnational and transcultural monetary zone laid the foundation for the unification of Afro-Asia, and eventually of the entire globe, into a single economic and political sphere.

People continued to speak mutually incomprehensible languages, obey different rulers and worship distinct gods, but all believed in gold and silver and in gold and silver coins.
之前提到了具有全球统一力的三样东西，金钱，政治和宗教。现在金钱在全球范围内已经可以统一了，同样的，政治和宗教的统一是大的历史的必然。

Once trade connects two areas, the forces of supply and demand trend to equalize the price of transportable goods.
一种东西可能在两个地方的价格不一样，但是如果两地之间有贸易的连接，那么很快，供求关系就会平衡这个货品在两地之间的价格。

Money is the only trust system created by humans that can bridge almost any cultural gap, and that does not discriminate on the basis of religion, gender, race, age or sexual orientation.
金钱可以跨越文化，它不歧视任何观念。

#### 10.5. The price of money
Money is based on two universal principles:
1. Universal convertibility
2. Universal trust.
Money has a dark side.

As money brings down the dams of community, religion and state, the world is in danger of becoming one big and rather heartless marketplace.

Brutal warriors, religious fanatics, and concern citizens have repeatedly managed to trounce calculating merchants, and even to reshape the economy. It is therefore impossible to understand the unification of humankind as a purely economic process. 
人类世界的整合并不完全是一个经济变化的过程。

### 11. Imperial visions
The ancient Numantians (努曼西亚人) are to this day Spain’s paragons of heroism and patriotism, cast as role models for the country’s young people.
努曼西亚人抵御外悔是现在西班牙人的精神典范，象征着英雄主义和爱国主义。

The victory of Roman over Numantia was so complete that the victors co-opted the very memory of the vanquished.

Most cultures have sooner or later fallen prey to the armies of ruthless empire, which have consigned them to oblivion. 
大多数的文明都逃脱不了被侵占被灭亡的命运。

Empires, too, ultimately fall, but they tend to leave behind rich and enduring legacies.
香港也是其中之一吗？从文化或者经济上面来讲。

#### 11.1 What is an empire?

文化的多元和不断扩张的领土是帝国的两大元素， 不然的话称不上是帝国。

An empire is defined solely by its cultural diversity and flexible borders, rather than by its origins, its form of government, its territorial extent, or the size of the population.
定义帝国两个决定性因素是文化的多元化，和不断扩张的领土。而不是他的来源，政府的组织形式，幅员辽不辽阔，人口多不多。

比如古代Athen和Aztec的疆域没有现在的希腊和墨西哥大，但是他们都征服了上百的政治实体。Athen征服了上百的城邦，Aztec的税收记录证明他们曾经管辖了371个不同的部落和民族。

古代的民族很多很小，占有的地盘比现在的名族要小很多。也说明了小文明被整合的历史趋势。

Empire were one of the main reasons for the drastic reduction in human diversity. The imperial steamroller gradually obliterated the unique characteristics of numerous people (such as Numantians), forging out of them new and much larger groups.
帝国是人类文明多样性降低的主要原因之一。

#### 11.2 Evil Empires?  帝国是邪恶的？
1. Empires do not work; 2. Every people has a right to self-determination
但是这两者都站不住脚。

Empire is a very stable form of organization.

The biological descendants of the people who had identified themselves as members of those nations, who had spoken their languages, worshipped their gods and told their myths and legends, now thought, spoke and worshipped as Romans.
文化被同化那些被征服的小民族的命运，即使帝国分崩离析以后，他们也都不可能再恢复了。

英国和法国的势力在中东消散以后，那些公元前8世纪被亚述人征服的小民族早就不在了。

现在犹太人讲的那些宗教上的法规和集会，其实在几千年前大卫王时代根本不存在。

The standard imperial toolkit included wars, enslavement, deportation, and genocide. 
帝国征服文明的四部曲，战争，奴役，放逐，和种族灭绝。

But to color all empires black and to disavow all imperial legacies is to reject most of human culture. Imperial elites used the profits of conquest to finance not only armies and forts but also philosophy, art, justice and charity.

Today most of us speak, think and dream in imperial languages that were forced upon our ancestors by the sword.
传神!

#### 11.3 It’s for your own good

Cyrus claimed not merely to rule the whole world, but to do so for the sake of all the people. Cyrus want the people he subjected to love him and to count themselves lucky to be Persian vassals.
统治者认为统治另外的文明是为了他们的好。

The presumption to rule the entire world for the benefit of all its inhabitants was startling.
但是这种为了全体的被统治者的好处的假设非常的不同寻常，令人震惊。

Evolution has made Homo Sapiens, like other social mammals, a xenophobic creature.
人类像其他的社交动物一样，都有仇视外族的本能。

Sapiens, instinctively, divide humanity into two parts, ‘we’ and ‘they’.
人类本能地区分自己和他人。在自己的语言里面，称呼自己是人，而别人就不是人。
中国也是一样的，称呼自己为华夏，华指的是衣服很美，夏指的是高雅，而在北方的少数民族就被称为“匈奴”。中国自称是中国，而别的国家自然就是周围的国家了。

In contrast with this ethnic exclusiveness, imperial ideology from Cyrus onward has tended to be inclusive and all-encompassing.
虽然有种族之间的差异，但是帝国的意思形态使得帝国可以包容一切。

Even though it has often emphasized racial and cultural differences between rulers and ruled, it has still recognized that basic unity of the entire world, the existence of a single set of principles governing all places and times, and the mutual responsibilities of all human being.
帝国的观念就是整个世界可以被统一起来，所有的地方所有的时间都有一个标准，而且人类之间有相互的责任。

这个帝国的观念也是美国一直不断的向全球推广他的民主观念和生活方式的内在驱动力吧？这是作为一个文化的帝国内在的属性。但是，从历史的角度来看，帝国来来去去，现在的帝国不一定一直存在，每个帝国所传播的理念必定是有利于他自身发展的。按照小文明合并变成大文明的历史进程，最后终将有一个全球化的大帝国，大文明。而谁能在这场竞争当中取胜，都不知道。现在显而易见的优劣地位，并不一定就是未来的趋势。作为中华，东方文明的一份子，当然希望自己的文明能够战胜，或者至少不被悲惨冷酷的同化掉。

同样的，也必须意识到，文化的宣传力非常的惊人，世界的每一个角落都有可口可乐，我们的正装都是西服，而自己的服饰却成了高雅的玩物。文化渗入得连自己都意识不到，反而接受这些，认为是平常的事情。文明之战的结果很难知晓。

不过有一点可以欣慰的是，中华文明的影响力，黏着力如此之强，以至于在美国这个文化的大熔炉里面，不少的中国人，仍然只在自己的小领地里面生活，和外面的世界隔绝。这是文化的异质性，还是种族的异质性在美国社会里面起作用? 像细菌被白细胞吞噬一样，我们的文明被吞噬以后，还能坚持这么长的时间，不能不让人内心钦佩。说自己融入不了主流社会的人们，你们是否太“自甘堕落”了？可以策略性的融入，以取得有利的条件，但是真的丢弃文明背景，是我们该做的吗？

检验一个文明的优质与否，可以有一个基于社会实验性质的判断：
1）作为小众在异质世界里面的生存力(持久的内生力，顽强的生命力)
2）作为本身对于异质世界的吸引力(文明的优质程度，物质的丰富程度，吸引力)

还有哪一个文明有如此强的生命力？我们的文明的优质程度还用怀疑吗？我们只是在历史的长河里落后一段时间，如果以千年记，这时只不过是一个浪尖后的浪谷。我们有必要那么自暴自弃，自惭形愧，以至于丢弃掉自己的本色吗？我想不会，也不可能，这个不是由少数人决定的，而是由文明内核决定的。

如果我们能够合适地掌握现代文明的游戏规则，掌握科学带来的生产力和管理方式的跨越式发展机会，再将我们的文明与之相融合，难道我们就没有机会吗？完全丢掉未来的想发是多么的懦弱和无知？如果仅仅是为了那么一些眼前的，物质的利益。

退一步来讲，个人有个人的选择，但是作为一个文明实体来讲，没有半点的退路。退路意味着就是消逝在历史的长河里，变成了在后代历史课本里面那一个曾经有过什么故事，但现在不见了的第1000零1个文明而已。



### 13. The secret of success.
Saying that a global society is inevitable is not the same as saying that the end result had to be the particular kind of global society now we have.

#### 13.1 The Hindsight Fallacy
It is an iron rule of history that what looks inevitable in hindsight was far from obvious at the time. Not that everything is possible. Geographical, biological and economic forces create constraints. Yet these constraints leave ample room for surprising developments, which do not seem bound by any deterministic laws.

Level 1 and Level 2 Chaos.
Leve 1 chaos is chaos that does not react to predictions about it. The weather prediction is level 1 chaos.
Level 2 chaos in chaos that will react to predictions about it, and therefore, can never be predicted accurately. The stock market, politics are level 2 chaos.
Unlike physics or economic, history is not a means for making accurate predictions.
古代的中国人很懂这个问题。“天数”，“天数”不可预测；也有“异数”的存在, 就是难以以当时人们的眼光来预测的黑天鹅事件。对于古代王朝治乱兴衰，有“气数”的说法， “气数”也是一个高层次的混沌问题。预测对于古代王朝政治的影响，有“谶纬之学”； 里面说的话都很模糊，所以在任何的时间的点都可以匹配上，如果被有心人利用，就会形成起义的导火索。同时，从另一方面不准的推论都可以预言的不确定性来搪塞，这样以来这些预言就不可能被证伪了。
被政治家利用，就变成鼓动人心的口号和工具，达到不可预估的效用。最有名的就是陈胜吴广的“大楚兴陈胜王”。历代封建王朝的统治者们都查禁谶纬之书，但是都会自觉不自觉的创造和继承一些预言，为自己所用。

#### 13.2 Blind Clio # Clio is the muse of history in ancient Greek
There is no proof that human well-being inevitably improves as history rolls along.
Different culture has different view of good. There is no proof that history is working for the benefit of humans because we lack an objective scale on which to measure such benefit.
Culture is very similar to virus. Christian heaven and communist paradise are the same. A culture idea can compel a human to dedicate his/her life to spreading that idea, even at the price of death. (政治理念也是一样的)

Human dies, but the idea spreads. 
The concept of “memetics”.
Organic evolution is based on the replication of organic information unites called “genes”, cultural evolution is based the replication of cultural information unites called “memes”. Successful cultures are those that excel in reproducing their memes, irrespective of the costs and benefits to their human hosts. 
(So some of Chinese cultural elements have the same “reproducing” magic and power to spread and strengthen itself? For example, the culture of family names and the worship to ancestor, these ideas will help the cultural children to remember firmly the cultural roots. China is more like a cultural country that was reinforced by the Han culture, but not a national country that is mainly grouped by the people with the same ethnic origin.) 黄道周更多的是殉死于大明道统，儒家忠君文化，而不是仅仅的一个皇帝。

The nationalist virus presented itself as being beneficial for humans, yet it has been beneficial mainly to itself. 虽然知道这个观点是对的，但是这个观点实在是难已接受。国家主义对个人没有意义，而只对其本身有意义。当然，没有各个人的国家主义，这个国家在外力的干预下就不会存在。这些主义的观念像病毒一样需要取食于载体，来完成它自己的存在。

那么从个人来讲，其实都是被观念驱使的。当外力逼迫到一定的地步，以至于这个观念要被灭亡时，它会驱使个人做出激烈的反应，比如香港年轻人的情况。

对于香港的年轻人来讲，回归前的旧香港要被灭亡了。但是，人还是那些人，新的香港，不接受也得接受。对于个体最好的选择就是接受。旧的观念不得不灭亡，在及其强大的外力下,由不得个人自己的选择。

但是，在文化之下，在观念之中的人，很难跳脱出来。被中华文化驱使的人，跑到了天涯海角，都还是被传统思想所束缚，所驱使（除了吃喝拉撒，你的喜怒哀乐，都是连接在文化上面的。我不知道拿着啤酒，站在一堆聊天吹牛有什么快乐。中国人的快乐，在听歌看戏，喝茶听奉承话）。

从大文化和小文化上面来讲，中国人和现在的香港人没有半点区别。反而，繁荣的经济，优质的教育，塑造出来高技能和高素质的香港人。中国人平均GDP如果高到香港的水平，生活的状态也应该和香港一样，只不过，不说广东话。

适应社会，适应文化，才是根本。适者生存。

Game theory explains how in multi-player systems, views and behavior patterns that harm all players nevertheless mange to take root and spread. “Arms racing” is a pattern of behavior that spreads itself like a virus from one country to another, harming everyone, but benefiting itself, under the evolutionary criteria of survival and reproduction.

No matter what you call it – game theory, postmodernism or memetics – the dynamics of history are not directed towards enhancing human well-being. 

History has a very wide horizon of possibilities, and many possibilities are never realized. 
改变历史的往往不是当时人们所了解的大事件，而是没有被当时的人们所了解的小事件。很多人都没有觉察到。
